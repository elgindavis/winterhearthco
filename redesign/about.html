Our Story

Hey there! I'm Elgin and I'm the human behind Winter Hearth Studios. I started this creative studio back in 2018 as a place for me to dream— to dream of ways that we can connect better with each other as humans and ways for us to live deeper, hyper-meaningful, and greatly fulfilling lives for ourselves.

Winter Hearth Studios is about being socially, mentally and emotionally aware, being energized for change, showing love in creative and impactful ways, and being a warm, safe and refreshing space to return to when the harsh, bitter winters of life rise up against us. We live in a pretty cold world, faced with adversity, struggle, and hardship. We can let those things control our thoughts and lives, or we can lift ourselves above them, conquer those fears, and live the lives we've always wanted to live.

You see, those challenges and struggles, those are the things that make us stronger. They make our stories more powerful. It's time to light a fire under the world and start making your own change. You want to make an app that supports PTSD victims? Build it. You want to get water and food to starving people in third-world countries? Make it happen. You want to change someone's life, showing them that they are not alone in whatever they are going through? Now's the time. We want to empower you as the hero/heroine of your own story — to slay the dragon, to defeat the Huns, to find true love, to save the world from an asteroid — wherever your life's story may take you. 

We want to inspire you to make a difference in the world — to challenge the status quo, to be the one to lend a helping hand, to create something that changes lives. We want you to live an epic story. We're building around those firestarters- those energetic, empathetic, optimistic individuals who have big dreams and are ready to light up the world with their passions.

We have a heart for change, and we will be that change we wish to see.

Behind the name

The idea behind Winter Hearth is to create a warm sense of home amidst the biting cold and chaos. The hearth is where the fire is. It's where we go to be restored, reinvigorated, and revitalized. It's where we find the power in our voices, the motivation to overcome, and the inspiration to create something awesome.
